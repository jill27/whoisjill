JILL SURROUNDING

Since Jill lives near the forest, it is always peaceful and quiet. She wakes up to the sound of birds chirping, as the morning sunrise seeps through her bedroom window, kissing her cheeks. As she open her bedroom window, she can smell the fresh air of wooden cupboard of old tea leaves, the wind brushing through her hair and softly against her face as the sun stares at her smiling
